Edtech startup TokSkill Social Learn Scoops Pre-Seed funding from Elina Startup Support Pvt Ltd.

TokSkill Learn: It is an online creative and maker courses platform in multi-languages.
This Bangalore-based Edtech startup TokSkill Social Learn has raised an undisclosed sum in the Pre-Seed funding round from Elina Startup Support Pvt Ltd. It plans to utilize the funds to invest in core business by strengthening its technology platform, and expand its business in India, with global ambitions.
The vision and mission of TokSkill Social Learn are to empower the young generation to become creators versus consumers of technology. And the community claims that all the creative and maker courses will be made available in Indian vernacular languages to make the process of learning easier. While the platform caters to the students in India, it also has global ambitions.
